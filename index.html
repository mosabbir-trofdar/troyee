About Troyee

Troyee Foundation is a social, non-political and non-profit organization founded by alumni of SSC Batch 2018, 2019 and 2020 of Kaunia Mofazzal Hossain Government Model High School. The aim and objective of this organization is to conduct various social activities by strengthening the mutual relations of the alumni of the three batches.
